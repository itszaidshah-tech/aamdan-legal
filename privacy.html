Privacy Policy

Aamdan Privacy Policy · Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to Aamdan ("Aamdan", "we", "our", or "us").

Aamdan is a social investing platform focused on the Pakistan Stock Exchange ("PSX"). The platform provides market information, portfolio tracking, watchlists, educational content, community discussions, calculators, alerts, and related investing tools.

Aamdan is not a brokerage, dealer, investment advisor, portfolio manager, financial institution, or custodian. We do not execute trades, hold client assets, or provide personalized financial advice.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect your information when you use the Aamdan mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By creating an account or using the Services, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Eligibility

Aamdan is intended only for individuals who are at least 18 years of age.

You may not use the Services if you are under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18.

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Username
  • Email address
  • Mobile phone number
  • Profile photograph
  • Login and authentication information

Profile Information

You may voluntarily provide:

  • Biography
  • Investing interests
  • Social links
  • Watchlists
  • Other profile details

Portfolio Information

You may voluntarily enter:

  • Stock holdings
  • Purchase prices

We collect content you create or share, including:

  • Watchlists you choose to share

Identity Verification Information

For certain features, including verification badges, fraud prevention, account security, regulatory compliance, brokerage partnerships, or enhanced trust features, we may request identity verification information.

This may include:

  • CNIC number
  • CNIC images (front and back)
  • Selfie or facial verification image
  • Date of birth
  • Verification status information

Identity verification information is collected only when you voluntarily apply for verification or when verification is required for a specific feature.

Identity verification information is stored separately from public profile information and is never visible to other users.

Location Information

If you choose to tag content with a location, we may collect approximate city-level location information.

We do not collect precise GPS coordinates unless specifically requested and authorized by you.

Device and Technical Information

We may automatically collect:

Analytics Information

We may collect information about:

  • Engagement metrics

This information helps us improve the Services and user experience.

4. AI Features

Certain features may use artificial intelligence technologies provided by third-party AI service providers, including Anthropic.

To support these features, limited information may be processed to generate summaries, insights, recommendations, educational content, search results, or other AI-powered functionality.

Before information is submitted for AI processing, we implement reasonable measures designed to remove or redact direct personal identifiers where practical, including:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • CNIC numbers
  • Financial account numbers

Identity verification information, including CNIC numbers, CNIC images, selfies, and government-issued identification documents, is never submitted to AI providers for processing.

AI-generated outputs may contain inaccuracies and should not be relied upon as investment, financial, legal, tax, or professional advice.
