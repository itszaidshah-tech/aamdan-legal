Privacy Policy

Aamdan · Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Aamdan ("Aamdan", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Aamdan mobile application (the "App"). Aamdan is a social investing platform for the Pakistan Stock Exchange (PSX) that provides market data, educational content, portfolio tracking, and community features. Aamdan is not a brokerage and does not execute trades.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what we need to run the App and the features you choose to use:

  • Account information: your name, email address, and phone number, used to create and secure your account and to sign you in.
  • Profile and user content: your username, bio, profile photo, posts, comments, polls, watchlists, and the holdings you choose to log or share.
  • Messages: the contents of chats you send to other users within the App.
  • Approximate location: if you choose to tag a post with a city, we use your device's approximate (city level) location. You can also select a city manually. We do not collect precise GPS coordinates.
  • Usage and diagnostics: interaction events (such as screens viewed and features used), app performance, and crash reports, used to keep the App working and to improve it.

We do not collect payment card numbers. Subscriptions are processed by Apple through In-App Purchase; we never see your card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the App and its features.
  • To create, secure, and manage your account.
  • To personalise your feed, recommendations, and content based on your interests and activity.
  • To analyse usage and improve performance, stability, and features.
  • To communicate with you about your account, important updates, and (if you opt in) news and offers.

3. We Do Not Sell Your Data or Track You Across Apps

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third-party advertising networks or data brokers, and we do not track you across other companies' apps and websites for advertising.

4. Sharing of Information

We share information only as needed to run the App:

  • With other users: your public profile, posts, comments, and shared content are visible to other users by design.
  • With service providers who process data on our behalf under contract, including our cloud database and storage provider (Supabase), analytics and crash reporting (Google Firebase), and subscription management (RevenueCat and Apple).
  • AI features: some features use a third-party AI provider (Anthropic) to generate summaries and insights. Before any content is sent for AI processing, we redact personal identifiers such as names, emails, phone numbers, national ID numbers, and financial account numbers. Raw personal data is not sent to the AI provider.
  • Legal: where required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on managed cloud infrastructure (Supabase) and protected in transit using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS). Sensitive credentials are stored securely on your device using the system keychain. While no system is perfectly secure, we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App. When you delete content or your account, that data is removed from our active systems, except where we are required to retain limited records to comply with legal obligations.

7. Your Choices and Rights

  • Access and update: you can view and edit your profile information in the App.
  • Delete your account: you can permanently delete your account from within the App. This removes your personal data and content from our active systems.
  • Permissions: you control camera, photo library, and location access through your device settings.
  • Notifications: you can manage notification preferences in the App and your device settings.

8. Children

Aamdan is intended for users aged 17 and older and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the applicable minimum age.

9. International Users

Aamdan is operated for users in Pakistan. Your information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country. By using the App, you consent to this processing.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you within the App.
